FAQ

Questions users will ask first

Are my PDFs uploaded anywhere?

No. Files stay inside your current browser session and are processed locally.

Can I remove just a few pages from a large PDF?

Yes. You can uncheck pages manually or apply a page range like 1-3,8.

Does merge order follow the file list on screen?

Yes. Use the move up and move down controls to decide the final merge order.

Why can large files feel slower?

Because rendering and merging happen locally in your browser instead of on a server.
